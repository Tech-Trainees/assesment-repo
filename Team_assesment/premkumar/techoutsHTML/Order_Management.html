Out-of-the-box order management functionalities for an eCommerce Mega store.

Our Client is an online Megastore that sells right from Apparel to electronics; their entire online store is built on Hybris Commerce and uses Adobe Analytics, Campaign and Target for engaging with customers. This case study focuses on a wide functionalities developed and integrated with Hybris OMS, making it a one of a kind Hybris OMS customizations in the eCommerce domain.

Background

Their platform is built on SAP Hybris Commerce; we have initially implemented SAP Hybris WCMS and OMS, integrated Adobe Analytics with their frontend system (Hybris WCMS).

They also use Adobe Campaign: we schedule promotions and lead generation campaigns through various channels such as SMSs, Emails, In-app notifications and recently adapting In-app messaging. We have also implemented Adobe Target and providing end-to-end support for these solutions.
