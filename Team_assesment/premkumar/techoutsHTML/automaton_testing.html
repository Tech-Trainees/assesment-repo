Techouts Test Management and Test Automation Services helped a leading Travel & tourism company Reduce Test Cycle Time and Achieve Timely Delivery

Project Summary

This case study focuses on various test scenarios executed for a largest travel & tourism company. A detailed architecture in the below section depicts the complexity of systems and a combination of manual and automation testing leveraged to determine and certify the system. Our approach was a hybrid QA approach and made sure the marketplace portal is ready for market and ultimately certifying if the data flow between systems is seamless.

About Client

Our client is one of the longest established travel companies having presence in UK, India, United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Netherlands, United Arab Emirates, Japan, Singapore, Australia and New Zealand.

Test cases feature

Payments & Finance, Booking, Inventory, Operations, Supplier Management portal and User management.

Key Business Challenges

Client has envisioned the next level of travel booking experience for all its end customers such as Agents, Portal customers, B2B, B2C, B2E, API clients etc. by building a high quality, high performance, very robust system to fulfil the need at every business vertical. The project Travelogixx 2.0 is the next-generation travel and booking platform envisioned by the client. This platform has various systems that come together to provide the customer or end user with unparalleled booking and travel planning convenience.

The platform is made up of several moving parts, each handling a separate business process. The main business entities are listed below.

  • MDM
  • Finance
  • Operations
  • Booking Engine
  • BRMS [Commercials, Offers, DTMS, Tax Engine]
  • Supplier Extranet [Private DB, Logistics & Cargos]
  • Supplier Integration

Travelogixx 2.0 covers following product & service flavours: Air [Flights], Accommodation [Hotels], Holidays, Packages, Activities, Cruise, Visa, Forex, Transfer, Rail, My Account etc.

It is multi-vendor development of the different modules and components. The biggest challenge for the client was to perform the end to end UAT testing, API testing, performance and automation testing to ensure the desired product quality is met. Techouts has helped the client to achieve all type of testing by integrating module wise SMEs [subject matter experts] and produce the transparent reports for each product and components.
