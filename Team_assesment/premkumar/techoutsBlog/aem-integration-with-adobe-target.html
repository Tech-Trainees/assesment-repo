AEM along with Adobe target for better customer reach:

Adobe Target is a personalization solution that makes it easy to identify and deliver your best content. It helps you create a targeted experience specific to each customer.

Adobe Target is built on the foundation of Adobe Experience Cloud (a collection of integrated online marketing and Web analytics products by Adobe) which integrates directly with Adobe core services including Adobe Launch (Tag management system), Adobe Experience Cloud Assets (Easily store, sync, and share all your digital assets in a central location, to save time and deliver a more consistent customer experience) and the Adobe Experience Cloud People core services (Create a comprehensive view of individual customers by connecting Adobe and non-Adobe data in your Experience Cloud solutions). These different core services provide information interchange between Adobe Experience Cloud solutions.
